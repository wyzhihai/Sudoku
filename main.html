Sudoku Quick Help

  • Sudoku is a puzzle game with numbers and a 9×9 grid.
  • The goal is to fill the entire grid with numbers from 1-9 so that no number repeats itself in
    any of the 9 rows,columns,or 9×3 houses.
  • Some numbers have been placed for you - this numbers cannot be changed.
